I’m TJ VanToll. I’m a developer advocate for Telerik, a jQuery team member, and author of jQuery UI in Action.

I speak about HTML5, web performance, and mobile development at conferences around the world, and I’ve written for publications such as Smashing Magazine, HTML5 Rocks, MSDN Magazine, and flippin’ awesome.
