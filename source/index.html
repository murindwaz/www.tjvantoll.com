I'm TJ VanToll. I'm a developer advocate for Telerik, a jQuery team member, and the author of jQuery UI in Action. I have over a decade of web development experience—specializing in performance and the mobile web.

I speak about my research and experiences at conferences around the world, and I've written for publications such as Smashing Magazine, HTML5 Rocks, MSDN Magazine, and ModernWeb.
