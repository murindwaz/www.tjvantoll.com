New Beginnings

Aug 14th, 2013 | Comments

I’m excited to announce that I’ll be joining Telerik as a Developer Advocate for Icenium. I’ve been doing a lot of speaking and writing in my free time, and this opportunity will give me a chance to do that as part of my day job.

While I’m excited, I’m also sad to leave behind some great people at Liquid Web. They were a great company and a fun place to work, but at the end of the day getting paid to speak and write has been a goal of mine, and this was an opportunity I couldn’t pass up.

I’ll be talking at two events in October - DevReach and HTML5DevConf. If you’re going to be there make sure to stop by and say hi.

Posted by TJ VanToll Aug 14th, 2013

TJ VanToll is a web developer, speaker, and writer living in Lansing, MI and working as a developer advocate for Telerik. He is an open source advocate that is on the jQuery UI team and publishes this blog open source. TJ is also the author of an upcoming book from Manning: jQuery UI in Action. When not online, TJ is generally found brainwashing his children and talking about himself in the third person.
